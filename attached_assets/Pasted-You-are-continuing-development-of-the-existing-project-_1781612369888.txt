IMPORTANT DESIGN PRINCIPLES
===========================

Invoices are immutable financial documents after payment.

At invoice generation time, snapshot all relevant information.

Historical invoices must never change when:

* Customer details change
* Subscription details change
* Plan pricing changes
* Company settings change

Never calculate historical invoices from current data.

Once any payment is received:

* Invoice becomes locked
* Invoice cannot be edited

If corrections are required after payment:

1. Cancel the existing invoice
2. Create a replacement invoice

==================================================
BUSINESS RULES
==============

Invoice generation:

* Only ACTIVE subscriptions can be invoiced

Payment rules:

* Payment amount must be greater than zero
* Total payments cannot exceed invoice total

Invoice status rules:

UNPAID:

paid_amount = 0

PARTIALLY_PAID:

0 < paid_amount < total_amount

PAID:

paid_amount >= total_amount

OVERDUE:

due_date < today AND balance_amount > 0

Cancelled invoices:

* Cannot accept payments
* Cannot be edited

==================================================
INVOICE EDITING RULES
=====================

Invoices are editable only when:

* Status = DRAFT OR UNPAID

AND

* paid_amount = 0
* is_locked = false

Editable fields:

* billing_period_start
* billing_period_end
* invoice_date
* due_date
* remarks

Do not allow editing:

* Company snapshots
* Customer snapshots
* Subscription reference
* Plan snapshots
* Pricing snapshots
* Invoice number

Once any payment is recorded:

* Set is_locked = true

Locked invoices:

* Cannot be edited
* Cannot be unlocked

Return:

409 Conflict

Message:

"Invoice cannot be modified because payment has already been recorded."
